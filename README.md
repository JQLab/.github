## Joint Quantum Systems Laboratory  
Led by Prof. Jianqing Liu

Research in:

- Quantum Networking  
- Quantum Internet Architectures

---

### 🔬 Research Vision

JQLab focuses on engineering-driven quantum systems research, 
bridging quantum information science and practical networked systems implementation.

We develop algorithms, protocols, and system prototypes for next-generation quantum-enabled networks and sensing platforms.
